Luxor 2-Days Package:
Top Attractions & Exciting Adventures

Visit the most exciting places in luxor within 2 days.

Transportation: Shuttle Service Included
Meals: Optional
Tickets: Book in advance
Languages: English, Spanish, French, Chinese, Japanese, Italian

Tour Overview

  • Explore ancient Luxor over 2 days.
  • Overnight stay on a traditional felucca boat.
  • Sunrise hot air balloon ride over Luxor.
  • Visit Valley of the Kings and Temple of Hatshepsut .
  • Tour Karnak and Luxor Temples.
  • Guided by a professional Egyptologist.
  • Air-conditioned transportation.
  • Included meals and traditional Egyptian tea on the felucca.
  • Blend of adventure, comfort, and culture.
  • Perfect for history enthusiasts and adventurers.

Highlights

  • Explore the Valley of the Kings with an Egyptologist guide.
  • Visit the magnificent Temple of Queen Hatshepsut.
  • Discover the Medinet Habu, the burial temple of Ramses III.
  • Admire the Colossi of Memnon, remnants of the Temple of Amenophis III.
  • Spend a unique night on a traditional felucca boat on the Nile.
  • Enjoy a sunrise hot air balloon ride over Luxor’s historical sites.
  • Tour the largest temple in the world, Karnak Temple.
  • Visit the Luxor Temple, built by Amenhotep III and completed by Ramses II.
  • Travel comfortably in a private air-conditioned vehicle.
  • Savor included meals and traditional Egyptian tea on the felucca.
  • All fees and taxes included.
  • The tour is fully customizable.

Itinerary

Day 1: West & East Bank

Valley of the Kings: Start your trip by picking up from your hotel and heading to the Valley of the Kings, guided by a professional Egyptologist.
Temple of Hatshepsut: Continue to the temple of Queen Hatshepsut.
Temple of Medinat Habu: Visit the burial temple of Ramses III.
Colossi of Memnon: Admire the impressive Colossi of Memnon.
Luxor: Transfer to the felucca for lunch, traditional Egyptian tea, and an unforgettable night on the Nile.
Karnak Temple: Visit the largest temple in the world.
Luxor Temple: Explore the Luxor Temple built by Amenhotep III and completed by Ramses II.
Accommodation: Overnight on the felucca.
Meals: Lunch, Dinner

Day 2: Sunrise Hot Air Balloon Ride

Sunrise Hot Air Balloon Ride: Begin with a dreamy hot air balloon ride over Luxor. Luxor: Transfer back to your hotel after the tour. Accommodation: Not included Meals: Not included
