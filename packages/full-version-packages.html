Tour Overview

See the best of Egypt in 10 days with this comprehensive tour which combines seven days of accommodation on a cruise plus three more nights in hotels. You’ll visit Luxor, Aswan, Hurghada, and Cairo, visiting major tourist attractions along the way– you’ll even get the chance to go on a hot air balloon ride over Luxor.
  • All meals are included in the price of the tour.
  • No hidden fees: admission tickets and activities are included.
  • A guide will accompany you throughout your journey.
  • This package includes seven nights’ accommodation on a cruise plus three nights in hotels.

Highlights

  • Arrival in Luxor, check-in, visit Luxor and Karnak Temples, dinner, and overnight on the cruise.
  • Early morning hot air balloon ride, visit Valley of the Kings, Temple of Hatshepsut, and statues of Memnon, lunch on the cruise, sail to Esna, overnight in Edfu.
  • Visit Edfu Temple by horse carriage, sail to Kom Ombo and visit the temple, then sail to Aswan.
  • Visit Philae Temple, High Dam, and Unfinished Obelisk, return to the cruise for lunch.
  • Early morning visit to Abu Simbel Temples, guided tour, return to the cruise for lunch.
  • Relax on the cruise and enjoy the atmosphere until arrival in Luxor.
  • After breakfast, check out and transfer by car to Hurghada, check-in at a five-star hotel.
  • Enjoy a Bedouin safari in the natural desert for a peaceful experience. Return to the hotel to prepare for departure.
  • After breakfast, enjoy a trip to Giftun Island, renowned for leisurely exploration of the Red Sea's marine life and scenic landscapes.
  • Depart from Hurghada to Cairo with our guide. Visit the Giza Pyramids, Sphinx, Saqqara, and ancient capital Memphis.
  • Transfer to the hotel for luggage retrieval and departure from Cairo Airport at 5 PM.
  • Full accommodation on the 5-stars boat for 7 days (breakfast, food and dinner) without drinks.
  • Transportation at tourist attractions by air-conditioned cars.
  • Reception and travel.
  • The tour is fully customizable.

Itinerary

Day 1: Luxor Arrival and East Bank Excursion

Morning: Welcome at Luxor train station or airport by our company representative. Transfer to the cruise boat to check in to your cabin by 11 AM. Afternoon: Enjoy lunch aboard the cruise. Explore Luxor Temple and Karnak Temple on the East Bank. Evening: Return to the cruise for dinner and overnight stay.

Admission: excluded

Day 2: Hot Air Balloon Ride and West Bank Discovery

Morning: Early breakfast followed by a thrilling hot air balloon ride over Luxor's vast outdoor museum. Guided tour of the West Bank with our knowledgeable company guide, visiting the Valley of the Kings, Temple of Hatshepsut, and the Colossi of Memnon. Afternoon: Return to the cruise for lunch. Sail past Esna Lock and continue to Edfu for an overnight stay.

Admission: excluded

Day 3 : Edfu Temple and Kom Ombo

Morning: After breakfast, embark on a carriage ride to visit Edfu Temple with our tour guide. Return to the boat for lunch and continue sailing to Kom Ombo Temple, exploring its unique dual dedication. Evening: Sail onward to Aswan for an overnight stay aboard the cruise ship.

Admission: excluded

Day 4 : Philae Temple, High Dam, and Unfinished Obelisk

Morning: Depart for Philae Temple, marvel at the High Dam, and explore the Unfinished Obelisk. Afternoon: Return to the boat for lunch and leisurely sailing on the Nile.

Admission: excluded

Day 5 : Abu Simbel Temple

Early Morning: Depart for Abu Simbel Temple, home to two impressive temples: the Great Temple dedicated to three gods and the smaller Hathor Temple dedicated to Nefertari. Morning: Explore the temples with detailed explanations from your friendly guide. Afternoon: Return to the boat for lunch and relaxation.

Admission: excluded

Day 6 : Relaxation Day

Day of Rest: Enjoy a leisurely day to soak in the atmosphere and relax until reaching Luxor.

Admission: excluded

Day 7 : Departure to Hurghada

Morning: After breakfast, check-out and disembark from the cruise. Transfer: Depart by car provided by the company to Hurghada. Accommodation: Arrive and check-in at a five-star hotel in Hurghada.

Admission: excluded

Day 8 : Safari Adventure

Morning: After breakfast, check-out from the hotel and store luggage. Safari Trip: Enjoy a Bedouin life experience in the natural desert, soaking in the calm and fresh air. Return: Return to the hotel in Cairo to prepare for departure.

Admission: excluded

Day 9 : Giftun Islands Excursion

Morning: After breakfast, depart for Giftun Island, renowned for its scenic beauty and abundant marine life in the Red Sea waters. Activities: Enjoy leisure time and explore the underwater world with its diverse sea creatures. Return: Return from the island in the afternoon.

Admission: excluded

Day 10 : Cairo Exploration - Last day (Party before Leaving Egypt)

Morning: Departure from Hurghada to Cairo with our company guide. Activities: Explore the iconic Giza Pyramids, Sphinx, Saqqara, and ancient capital of Memphis. Afternoon: Return to Cairo hotel to collect luggage. Evening: Departure from Cairo Airport at 5 am. This summarizes the itinerary for your last day, focusing on the journey from Hurghada to Cairo and the sights you'll visit.

Admission: excluded
