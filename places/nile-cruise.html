Nile Cruise

3, 4, 7 Nights Nile Cruise with Hot Air Balloon Experience.

Optional

Tickets:

Book in advance

Languages:

Tour Overview

  • Relax on a Luxury Cruise: Enjoy the comfort and elegance of a luxury cruise as you sail along the Nile River, the lifeblood of ancient and modern Egypt.
  • Extraordinary Journey Through Ancient Egypt: A Nile cruise from Luxor to Aswan, or vice versa, offers a rich experience of Egypt's history, passing iconic archaeological sites and breathtaking Nile views.
  • Starting in Luxor – Open-Air Museum: Beginning in Luxor, known as the "world's greatest open-air museum," the cruise includes visits to the Temples of Karnak and Luxor, the Valley of the Kings, and the Temple of Queen Hatshepsut.
  • Ancient Architecture and Intricate Carvings: Each site showcases Egypt's religious and royal practices, featuring monumental architecture and detailed carvings that vividly illustrate ancient history.
  • Scenic Nile Views Along the Way: Enjoy stunning views of the lush Nile valley, set against desert landscapes as the cruise makes its way south.
  • Stops at Historic Temples: The itinerary includes the Temple of Edfu, dedicated to Horus, and the Temple of Kom Ombo, a unique dual temple honoring both Sobek and Horus.
  • Aswan’s Historical Significance: Aswan offers landmarks like the Philae Temple, dedicated to Isis, and the Aswan High Dam. Optional excursions to the awe-inspiring Abu Simbel Temple complex are also available.
  • Onboard Luxury and Amenities: Passengers enjoy modern amenities, such as pools, gourmet dining, and entertainment, blending relaxation with cultural exploration.
  • A Memorable Cruise Experience: Whether cruising from Luxor to Aswan or the reverse, this journey combines luxury with cultural immersion along Egypt's timeless river.
