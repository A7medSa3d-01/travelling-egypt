Karnak Temple

The Largest temple in Egypt.

Transportation:

Limousine or Toyota high s for Private packages and bus for Group

Meals:

Optional

Tickets:

Book in advance

Languages:

English, Spanish, French, Chinese, Japanese, Italian, and Arabic

Tour Overview

  • Explore the Largest Religious Complex: Visit one of the largest and most significant religious complexes in the world, dedicated to the god Amun-Ra.
  • Admire Monumental Architecture: Marvel at the massive pylons, monumental gateways, and intricately carved statues and obelisks that exemplify ancient Egyptian architecture.
  • Walk Through the Hypostyle Hall: Experience the awe-inspiring Hypostyle Hall, featuring 134 towering columns adorned with detailed hieroglyphics depicting pharaohs and gods.
  • Visit the Sacred Lake: Discover the Sacred Lake, historically used for purification rituals, adding to the temple's spiritual significance.
  • Learn About Pharaoh Contributions: Understand the contributions of various pharaohs, including Ramses II, Hatshepsut, and Amenhotep III, each leaving their unique mark on the temple.
  • Discover Historical Significance: Gain insights into the spiritual and political importance of Karnak Temple in ancient Egyptian society, making it an essential part of your exploration.
